Discover Paris: The City of Light

Paris, France’s capital, is a dream destination blending art, history, and cuisine. Here are some highlights:

  • Eiffel Tower: Built in 1889 for the World’s Fair, this iconic 330-meter tower offers stunning views.
  • Louvre Museum: Home to the Mona Lisa, it’s the world’s largest art museum, founded in 1793.
  • Notre-Dame: A Gothic masterpiece from 1163, famous for its French Gothic architecture.

Historical Fact: Did you know Paris was once a Roman settlement called Lutetia, founded in the 3rd century BC?

Try a croissant at a local café or stroll along the Seine for an unforgettable experience.
